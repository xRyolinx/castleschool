The Castle School

Community

For 12 years, The Castle School has been a beacon of education across seven locations in Algeria. We take pride in offering a diverse range of courses both on-site and online, empowering students to unlock the doors to the world through language learning.

The Castle School is present in 7 Wilayas including : Oran, Blida, Algiers, Boumerdes, Bejaia, Setif and Constantine.

With additional campuses across 4 regions in Algiers: Algiers Center, Dely Brahim, Bab Ezzouar, and Draria.

Our multilingual learning experience is conveniently accessible across Algeria.

Our Programs

Face-to-Face Program

Where students engage in dynamic classroom environments tailored to their language learning needs. Our expert instructors utilize interactive teaching methods to foster language proficiency and cultural understanding. With small class sizes and personalized attention, students thrive in an environment that encourages participation and active learning.

Online Program

Our virtual classrooms offer interactive lessons led by experienced instructors, ensuring effective learning from the comfort of your own home. With customizable schedules and comprehensive course materials, students receive the same quality education as our face-to-face program.
